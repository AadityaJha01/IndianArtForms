History Of Indian Art Forms

Indian art and architecture have evolved over thousands of years, shaped by diverse cultures, religions, and historical influences. From the prehistoric cave paintings at Bhimbetka and the sophisticated city planning of the Indus Valley Civilization to the Mauryan Empire's stone architecture, Indian art has continuously developed. The Gupta period saw the rise of Hindu temple architecture, while the 12th century introduced Indo-Islamic styles, epitomized by the Taj Mahal. The Mughal era enriched Indian art with miniature paintings and grand forts, while British colonial rule brought European influences, creating a unique blend that continues today.
